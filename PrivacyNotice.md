### 一、隐私说明（中文版）

本隐私说明适用于我们提供的 `automaticFilling` 浏览器扩展（以下简称“本扩展”）。我们非常重视您的隐私和个人信息保护。

#### 1. 我们如何收集和使用信息

1. **本地表单数据**
    - 收集内容：您在本扩展中自行保存的表单信息（如姓名、地址、邮箱等）。
    - 使用目的：仅用于自动填充网页表单，以提高您的填写效率。

2. **使用数据（如有启用）**
    - 可能收集的匿名统计信息包括：功能使用频率、错误日志等。
    - 仅用于改进本扩展性能和用户体验，不会用于识别个人身份。

3. **不收集的内容**
    - 除非您主动输入并保存在本扩展中，我们不会擅自读取或收集您网页中的内容。
    - 我们不会刻意收集敏感个人信息（如身份证号、银行卡号、密码等）。如您自行将此类信息保存在本扩展中，请自行权衡风险。

#### 2. 数据存储与安全

1. **存储位置**
    - 您的表单数据默认保存在浏览器本地存储中（如 `chrome.storage` 等），不自动上传到我们的服务器。

2. **安全措施**
    - 我们采用合理的技术手段（如本地加密、最小权限访问）保护数据安全。
    - 但互联网环境存在风险，我们无法保证绝对安全，请您妥善管理设备和浏览器权限。

#### 3. 数据共享与第三方服务

1. **不出售、不交易**
    - 我们不会出售、出租或以其他方式交易您的个人信息。

2. **第三方服务（如适用）**
    - 如本扩展中集成第三方服务（例如同步、备份或分析工具），我们会在设置或说明中明确标注，并尽量减少传输的个人数据。
    - 第三方对信息的处理遵守其各自的隐私政策。

#### 4. 您的权利

您可以在浏览器扩展的设置中：

- 查看或修改您已保存的表单数据；
- 删除单条数据或清空本扩展中的全部数据；
- 随时在浏览器中禁用或卸载本扩展。

#### 5. 未成年人保护

本扩展主要面向具备完全民事行为能力的用户。如您为未成年人，请在监护人指导下使用本扩展，并避免保存敏感个人信息。

#### 6. 本隐私说明的更新

我们可能会根据功能或法律法规的变化适时更新本隐私说明。重大变更时，我们会通过扩展更新说明或其他合理方式提示您。更新后的隐私说明自发布之日起生效。

### 二、Privacy Notice (English Version)

This Privacy Notice applies to the `automaticFilling` browser extension (hereinafter referred to as “the Extension”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

#### 1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

1. **Local Form Data**
    - Information collected: Form data that you voluntarily save in the Extension (such as name, address, email, etc.).
    - Purpose of use: Only to auto-fill web forms and improve your efficiency.

2. **Usage Data (if enabled)**
    - We may collect anonymous statistics such as feature usage frequency and error logs.
    - This data is used solely to improve performance and user experience and is not used to identify you personally.

3. **Information We Do NOT Intentionally Collect**
    - Unless you explicitly input and save it in the Extension, we do not arbitrarily read or collect the content of the web pages you visit.
    - We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as ID numbers, bank card numbers, passwords, etc.). If you choose to store such data in the Extension, you do so at your own discretion and risk.

#### 2. Data Storage and Security

1. **Storage Location**
    - Your form data is stored locally in your browser (e.g., via `chrome.storage`) and is not automatically uploaded to our servers.

2. **Security Measures**
    - We adopt reasonable technical measures (such as local encryption and minimal access permissions) to protect your data.
    - However, given the nature of the Internet, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please manage your devices and browser permissions carefully.

#### 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

1. **No Selling or Trading of Personal Data**
    - We do not sell, rent, or otherwise trade your personal information.

2. **Third-Party Services (if applicable)**
    - If the Extension integrates third-party services (for example, sync, backup, or analytics tools), we will clearly indicate this in the settings or documentation and will minimize the personal data transmitted.
    - Any information handled by third parties is subject to their own privacy policies.

#### 4. Your Rights

Within the Extension settings, you may:

- View or modify the form data you have saved;
- Delete individual entries or clear all data stored by the Extension;
- Disable or uninstall the Extension from your browser at any time.

#### 5. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is intended for users with full civil capacity. If you are a minor, please use the Extension under the guidance of a guardian and avoid storing sensitive personal information.

#### 6. Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time to reflect changes in features or legal requirements. For material changes, we will notify you via Extension update notes or other reasonable means. The updated Privacy Notice will take effect upon publication.
